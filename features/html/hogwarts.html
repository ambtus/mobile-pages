Chapter 2: Hogwarts

Notes:

(See the end of the chapter for notes.)

Chapter Text

Amy looked at the pair in disbelief. "Hogwarts. I see," she said flatly. "Look," she sighed. "I think this has gone on long enough. Who are you really? What do you want with me?" Her voice raising slightly she continued, "And why are you pretending that Harry Potter really existed."

The wizard frowned at her. "He doesn't," he said. "He should, but something has gone wrong."

"When he missed his conception date, alarms went off all over the Department for the Prevention of Temporal Interference," the witch added.

Amy just laughed incredulously and shook her head. "You two are really odd, you know that? Not that I believe you, but even if he did (and how can someone miss their own conception?), what would it have to do with me?"

They looked at each other. "We don't know," said the wizard. "All we know is that the Tempora Peragro spell pinpointed a prophecy with you as it's focus."

Amy raised her eyebrows. "There's a Prophecy about me?" she asked. "That's ridiculous. I'm not a witch." Neither of her visitors would meet her gaze. "I'm not. Am I?"
