Chapter 1: One Torch, Burning Bright

Chapter Text

Itachi stared blankly at the empty cliff, blood drip-drip-dripping from the eye in his hand.

-

Itachi made a deal with Danzo Shimura.

-

Itachi cut down Uchiha after Uchiha.

-

Itachi ran away.

-

Itachi made a deal with Hiruzen Sarutobi.

-

Itachi left Sasuke alone.

-

Itachi gave his friendbrotherfriendcousin to a crow.

~~~

Weeks earlier.

~~~

Shisui’s breath pushed out in a breath of bubbles, the water cold. They instinctively reached out with chakra-sensing, the water twisting and flipping him around, completely disorienting him to the point that even with their eyes, he was fairly sure that he would be completely lost.

I’m sorry, Itachi.

Shisui poured their chakra into the blood-laced waters as weight filled his lungs.

~~~

And Uzushio Answers Her Children.

And Uzushio Knows Her Allies.

And Uzushio Misses Her People.

And Uzushio Notices Her Torches.

And Uzushio Laments Her Loss.

And Uzushio Is Her Water.

And Uzushio Sees Her Chance.

And Uzushio Takes Her Risks.



And Uzushio Reaches Her Hope.




And Uzushio Knows Her Deaths.




~~~

Something rough rubbed at Shisui’s cheek, sandpaper-like.

-

“The first in so long to give me a chance to bring them home… and it’s my last Torch…”

-

Shisui slowly turned his head, following the rustling of something he didn’t know.

“Are you awake enough now, little Torch?”

Shisui opened his mouth to respond, but only a croak of air came out.

The voice laughed. “Well you did just about drown before I got to you! Sleep, and the next time you’re awake we’ll see how you’re doing.”

-

Shisui awoke again, in as far as he could tell the same place as the last time.

“Ah, you’re back again!” The same voice.

“ Who are you…? ” Shisui’s voice was still weak.

The voice chuckled. “I am Uzushio.”

“ What? ”

“Well, My current residents are not well-equipped to assist a half-drowned Human in recovering, so I had to get creative!”

“ Current… residents? ”

Uzushio laughed. “The animals!”

-

Shisui had made his way around the space he was in, getting at least a vague idea of the room. There was the bed they’d been recovering on, a closed window, a door, and a table on the side.

Outside the house, there was sand and what smelled and felt like a cooking pit, with two flat stones as chairs, and Shisui could hear the ocean not far off.

So. He was really on Uzushio.

Being cared for by Uzushio.

-

“Why me?”

“You are a Torch, are you not?”

The… title was slightly familiar, but Shisui couldn’t fully place it. “What does that mean?”

“The Cat contract is from my shores,” Uzushio explained calmly, stirring whatever was in their bowl. “Matatabi and I gifted it to an Uchiha back before the Leaves were founded, for use only as peace. They dubbed themself a Torch. Your Kagami was the last for a short while until he passed it on to you. Now you are the last.”

Oh. That made some sense. “Why not take an Uzumaki?”

Uzushio sighed sadly. “It is as the push and pull of the tides. I can look out and send calls through the air and the wind, but I can only pull Mine directly to me through the water, and so far inland only in distress. The little Fox has not given me an opportunity to bring him, nor have any of the others.”

Shisui hummed, peeling a strip of his fish off and eating it. “How long… how long will I have to stay here?”

“I will not force you to stay, now that you are recovered as much as you will be.”

“How long has it been? ”

Uzushio hummed. “I would say… nearly a moon cycle, give or take.”

Shisui’s head cocked slightly. It should have taken him far longer to heal than it had.

Uzushio laughed again. “You are one of Mine, and I was able to heal you rather quickly.”

-

“How do I get back to the mainland?”

“It’s to the west.” Uzushio pointed Shisui’s arm in a direction, presumably west.

-

Shisui nodded gratefully to Uzushio. “I’ll be back, I’m sure.”

Uzushio laughed. “I do hope so!”

Shisui summoned Mikka to help be their ‘eyes’, turning westward. “Thank you.”

-

Shisui was very glad for Mikka on their shoulders. Being blinded was already massively stressful. Add onto that being blinded in the middle of the ocean after nearly drowning while blinded, and the warm fur of the cat was almost all that was keeping Shisui grounded.

Shisui took another deep breath, feet pounding over the water. A straight line. Run in a straight line.
