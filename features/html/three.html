Cold Water, Unsnuffed Torch

LesbianOzoneLizard

Chapter 3: Run Boy Run

Notes:

cw: gutting of a squirrel, eyeball stuff

Chapter Text

Shisui sighed, two extremely tired kids pressed to either side in the small hollow they’d found. He wasn’t sure exactly how far from Konoha he was, but now he had two problems: his preservation storage seal was occupied by three eyeballs, so his food wouldn’t last as long, and with one extra mouth he wouldn’t have enough food regardless.

And Shisui really didn’t feel like rationing the kids down on food.

So Shisui planned.

Clearly, there would be none of Shisui getting his eye back for the time being.

Shisui also currently had three extra Sharingan in his possession.

Kagami Uchiha. Either Amaterasu or the unknown eye that he’d never told anyone about the ability of, which could be an issue as Shisui had no way to tell the two eyes apart.

Well… no humans had known what Kagami’s other eye had been.

“Mikka, who would know Kagami’s other Mangekyo ability?”

Shisui chose one of the two of Kagami’s eyes at random. He either got Amaterasu or Terekineshisu, which he was not testing while on the run and needing all his chakra to get to the shore.

Shisui ran on.

-

It took four days for Shisui and the two kids to reach the ocean.

Shisui made quick work of finding a cave. He currently was hungry, and had no way back to Uzushio until he replenished his chakra, and even then he was pretty sure that he wouldn’t be able to get both kids across the water.

“Shi-nii, why are we here?”

Right. Shisui hadn’t actually explained to Sasuke why they were going to the ocean. He sighed. “I have… a friend out here. She’s how I survived. Once I’ve recovered, we’re going to her, and we’ll be safe.”

“But… what about Ita-nii?”

I don’t know. Shisui smiled, ruffling Sasuke’s hair. “She’ll help, if only with supplies. Now…” They turned to look at Naruto, smirking slightly. “I am in need of a good nap. Naruto, you know how to set traps, yes?”

Naruto nodded. “Yep, ‘ttebayo!”

Sasuke’s eyes widened at the blonde.

“Good.” Shisui pointed out the mouth of the cave. “We’re going to be staying here for a bit. Can you set up some to protect us, please?”

“Can… I help?”

Naruto grinned at Sasuke. “Yeah! I’ll show you all my best traps, dattebayo!”

Shisui was very grateful that Sasuke and Naruto were getting along now. It would have been an even longer four days otherwise.

-

Shisui was coaching Naruto and Sasuke on cleaning the squirrels that Naruto had- honestly very skillfully- caught.

Naruto threw his kunai down into the sand, frustrated. “Why can’t I just do this my way, ‘ttebayo?!”

Sasuke blinked. “‘Your way’?”

“Yeah!”

Shisui was curious. They’d never seen Naruto catch any animals, much less clean a kill. “Alright Naruto, what’s your way?”

Naruto’s face scrunched in annoyance. “Y’can’t freak out, alright? Gotta promise, ‘ttebayo!”

Shisui picked up the discarded kunai, setting it on the mostly-cleaned rock being used as a table. “I think we can manage that.”

“Promise, Naru,” Sasuke nodded.

Naruto waited a moment longer before closing his eyes. The hand that held his squirrel tensed slightly, and his nails thickened and sharpened into black claws.

Shisui’s eyes widened.

Within minutes, Naruto had the squirrel gutted, and the various organs in two neat piles: one of the digestive tract, heart, and lungs, and the other of the brain, liver, and a few other organs. Naruto pointed at the pile with the liver, fingers still clawed and now slightly stained with blood: “Good.” To the pile with the heart: “Not as good.” To the squirrel’s body: “Very good.”

Shisui blinked. He almost did not want to know how Naruto could do that so cleanly.

“Why with your fingers?” Sasuke asked.

Naruto shrugged. “None of my kunai were sharp enough to be any good, ‘ttebayo. So they taught me how to use my claws.”

“‘They’?” Shisui asked.

“They.” Naruto offered no more information.

Shisui decided not to pry.

-

It turned out that Naruto was a natural at ocean-running.

Shisui was very glad for this, as he was nowhere near recovered enough to carry both kids all the way over the ocean to Uzushio.

~~~

Kakashi half-stumbled to an exhausted halt on the sand, staring at the three shapes racing away over the water. There was absolutely no way that it wasn’t who Kakashi was looking for.

Shisui Uchiha. Sasuke Uchiha. Naruto Uzumaki.

One supposed to be dead. One assumed dead by most of Konoha. One kidnapped by the one who was supposed to be dead.

Kakashi once again gave chase.
